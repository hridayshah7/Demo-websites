The Craftsman

Meet Chef Arjun Nair

With roots in Kerala's rich culinary heritage and training at Le Cordon Bleu, Chef Arjun Nair brings a unique perspective to Indian cuisine. His philosophy is simple: honor tradition while embracing innovation.

Over 15 years, he has cooked in kitchens across three continents, absorbing techniques and flavors before returning to India to create Saffron Kitchen -- a restaurant where every dish is a letter of love to his homeland.
