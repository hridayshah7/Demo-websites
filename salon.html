The Artist Behind the Glow

London Trained 8+ Years Vogue India Celebrity Clients

Meera Kapoor

Meera trained at one of London's most prestigious academies before returning to India with a vision: to bring world-class beauty artistry to Bangalore. Over eight years, she has built Luxe Glow into Indiranagar's most sought-after beauty destination, with a clientele that includes Bollywood personalities, tech CEOs, and brides from across the country.

Her work has been featured in Vogue India, and she is known for her ability to create looks that are both timeless and contemporary. Whether it's a subtle balayage or a red-carpet transformation, Meera approaches each client as a unique canvas.

"Beauty is deeply personal. My role is to listen, understand, and then create something that makes you feel like the most radiant version of yourself."
