Meet Your Dentist

Dr. Priya Sharma

BDS, MDS (Prosthodontics)

Dr. Priya Sharma is one of Bandra's most trusted dental professionals, known for her gentle approach and meticulous clinical skill. After completing her BDS and MDS from Mumbai University with a Gold Medal in Prosthodontics, she founded SmileCare Dental with a simple mission: make quality dental care accessible, painless, and genuinely pleasant.

With over 12 years of hands-on experience, she has treated thousands of patients across all age groups, specializing in smile makeovers, dental implants, and full-mouth rehabilitation. She regularly attends international conferences to stay at the forefront of dental innovation.
